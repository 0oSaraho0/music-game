Welcome to National Anthems!

Arguably the best National Anthems based quiz on the internet!

Here is how you play

  1. Click the play game button on the home page
  2. Click the play button at the top of the screen
  3. listen to the national anthem
  4. click on the area of the map you think the anthem is from

It's as simple as that!

If you get the question right you will see a congratulations banner
at the bottom of the screen and your score will increment by 1.

If you are incorrect you will also be notified a the bottom of the screen
and the correct country will colour black.

Please sign in to check out the leader board and see how you have scored againt other players!
